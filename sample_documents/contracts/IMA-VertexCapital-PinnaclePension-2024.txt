SECTION 2: FEES & ECONOMICS
================================================================================
2.1 Management Fee:
    2.0% per annum on committed capital during investment period (3 years)
    1.5% per annum on invested capital during harvest period
    
    Investor's management fee (INR 200 Cr × 2%): INR 4.0 Crore per annum
    Quarterly billing: INR 1.0 Crore per quarter

2.2 Performance Fee (Carried Interest):
    20% of profits above the Hurdle Rate
    Hurdle Rate (Preferred Return): 10% IRR per annum (compounded annually)
    Catch-up: 100% catch-up to Manager once hurdle is cleared
    High-water mark: Applies — no carry on previously distributed amounts

    Illustrative Economics (assuming 18% gross IRR on INR 200 Cr, 5 years):
    Gross Return                           : ~INR 490 Crore
    Less: Management Fees (5 years)        : (INR 19.0 Crore)
    Net Return before carry                : ~INR 471 Crore
    Preferred Return to Investor (10% IRR) : ~INR 122 Crore (returned first)
    Remaining Profit                       : ~INR 349 Crore
    Manager Carry (20%)                    : ~INR  69.8 Crore
    Net to Investor                        : ~INR 401.2 Crore
    Net IRR to Investor                    : ~15.3%

2.3 Other Fees:
    Transaction fees (deal sourcing): 1% of deal value (shared 50:50 with Fund)
    Monitoring fees: Charged to portfolio companies (100% to Fund)
    Broken deal costs: To Fund (capped at INR 50 lakh per annum)

SECTION 5: RISK DISCLOSURES
================================================================================
IMPORTANT: The following risk factors are disclosed pursuant to SEBI AIF
Regulations 2012 and must be read carefully by all investors:

5.1 LIQUIDITY RISK: Fund investments are in unlisted/illiquid securities.
    No secondary market exists for most investments. Investor should be
    prepared to hold for the full Fund life (7+ years).

5.2 LEVERAGE & DEBT RISK: While the Fund does not employ leverage, portfolio
    companies may have significant debt. High leverage (D/E > 3x) in portfolio
    companies increases risk of capital loss. Three current investments
    (representing 22% of corpus) have leverage >3x.

5.3 CONCENTRATION RISK: The Fund's real estate and infrastructure exposure
    at 28% exceeds the 25% guideline. Manager is actively rebalancing.

5.4 PERFORMANCE RISK: Past performance is not indicative of future results.
    The Fund's current IRR (16.2%) is below the 20-25% target range.
    Two stressed investments may result in partial capital impairment.

5.5 REGULATORY RISK: SEBI, RBI, FEMA, and tax regulations are subject to
    change and could adversely affect Fund operations and investor returns.

5.6 CURRENCY RISK: Minimal (India-focused Fund). Any offshore investments
    require RBI approval and create USD/INR exposure.
