SECTION 2: SCOPE OF SERVICES
================================================================================
2.1 The Provider agrees to deliver the following services to the Client:
    a) Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) implementation (SAP S/4HANA)
    b) Financial reporting and analytics platform (customized for IGAAP & Ind AS)
    c) Treasury management system with real-time cash flow dashboards
    d) Regulatory compliance module (SEBI, RBI, MCA, GST integrations)
    e) API connectivity to NSE, BSE, and RBI data feeds
    f) 24×7 technical support with 4-hour critical issue response SLA

2.2 All services shall be delivered from Provider's SOC-2 Type II certified
    infrastructure hosted on AWS Mumbai Region (ap-south-1).

SECTION 4: FINANCIAL RISK PROVISIONS & LIABILITY
================================================================================
4.1 Limitation of Liability:
    Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed
    the fees paid in the preceding 12 months (INR ~1.6 Crore for Year 1).
    This cap does not apply to: (a) death/personal injury, (b) gross
    negligence/wilful misconduct, (c) breach of confidentiality.

4.2 Indemnification:
    Each Party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims arising
    from its own acts, omissions, or IP infringement. Provider specifically
    indemnifies Client against any claims of IP infringement in the software.

4.3 Financial Default Events:
    Client default triggers if:
    a) Payment overdue by more than 45 days
    b) Client files for insolvency under IBC 2016
    c) Client's credit rating falls below BBB- (investment grade threshold)
    d) Change in Control of Client without 60-day prior written notice

    Upon default, Provider may:
    i)  Suspend services with 15-day notice
    ii) Demand accelerated payment of all remaining contract value
    iii) Terminate this Agreement and seek damages

4.4 Escrow Arrangement:
    Source code shall be held in a mutually agreed escrow with a NASSCOM
    accredited agent. Release triggers: Provider insolvency, material breach
    uncured within 60 days, or cessation of Provider's business.

SECTION 5: DATA PROTECTION & FINANCIAL DATA SECURITY
================================================================================
5.1 Data Classification:
    All financial data processed under this Agreement is classified as
    "RESTRICTED" under Provider's Data Classification Policy v3.2.

5.2 Data Residency:
    All Client data shall be stored exclusively within India (AWS ap-south-1).
    No cross-border data transfer without express written consent.

5.3 Security Standards:
    Provider shall maintain: ISO 27001:2022 certification, SOC 2 Type II
    compliance, PCI-DSS compliance (where applicable), and DPDP Act 2023
    compliance from the effective date of its notification.

5.4 Audit Rights:
    Client has the right to conduct one security audit per calendar year
    with 30-day advance notice. Provider shall cooperate fully.

5.5 Breach Notification:
    In case of a data breach involving Client's financial data, Provider
    shall notify Client within 72 hours and CERT-In within prescribed timelines.

SECTION 7: TERMINATION
================================================================================
7.1 Either Party may terminate for cause with 30-day cure notice.
7.2 Client may terminate for convenience with 90-day notice and payment of
    termination fee equal to 3 months of applicable recurring fees.
7.3 Upon termination, Provider shall export all Client data in standard
    formats (CSV, PDF, XML) within 30 days at no additional charge.
7.4 Financial Consequences of Termination:
    - If terminated by Client (convenience): Termination fee applies
    - If terminated by Client (Provider breach): All advance payments refunded
    - If terminated by Provider (Client default): Accelerated payment clause applies
